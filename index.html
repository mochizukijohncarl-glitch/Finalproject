Where Great Food Meets Great Views...

SAND DUNES SNACK HOUSE

Sand Dunes Snack House is your go-to stop for delicious, freshly prepared snacks right at the heart of the Laoag Sand Dunes adventure zone. Whether you're gearing up for a thrilling ride or cooling down after sandboarding, we’re here to give you a quick, satisfying, and affordable food experience.

We specialize in crowd-favorite snacks such as burgers, lomi, empanada, siomai, lumpia, maruya, and refreshing beverages—perfect for travelers, families, and adventure seekers. At Sand Dunes Snack House, we serve comfort food that keeps your energy up and your mood high.

OUR CORE VALUES

Cleanliness

We maintain a clean and organized environment to ensure the safety and comfort of all our customers. A clean space means a better experience for everyone.

Quality

We believe that great food starts with fresh ingredients and careful preparation. Every snack we serve is made to deliver satisfying flavor and consistent quality.

Consistency

We strive to deliver the same great taste, quality, and service every single day. No matter when you visit, you can count on us for a reliable snack experience.

Why Choose Us

Sand Dunes Snack House is more than just a food stop, it’s part of your adventure. Guests love us for our friendly service and consistent quality that keeps people coming back. We provide a cool spot to relax, recharge, and enjoy good food before or after exploring the iconic Laoag Sand Dunes. When you crave great taste and great value, we’re always here to serve you.
